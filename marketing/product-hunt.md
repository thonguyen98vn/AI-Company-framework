# Product Hunt Launch: AI Company Framework V3

**Tagline:** Turn your AI IDE into a fully-autonomous Virtual Tech Company.

**Description:**
Tired of prompting Claude, Cursor, or Copilot line by line? The AI Company Framework V3 is an OS-level paradigm shift for agentic coding. By scaffolding a virtual `.agents` directory, you instantly hire an Architect, Coder, and Auditor to build your project.

Key Features:
- 🏗 **Founder Interview:** The AI PM asks you questions and locks in the architecture before writing a single line of code.
- 🔀 **State Machine & Events:** Agents asynchronously hand off tasks (e.g., Architect -> Coder) via an internal event log.
- 🧩 **Domain Presets:** Instant setups for Mobile, Web3, GameDev, SaaS, and Data Science.
- 📊 **Live Dashboard:** Visualize what your AI agents are doing in real-time on a beautiful Next.js dashboard.
- 🛠 **Multi-IDE Support:** Works with Cursor, Windsurf, Copilot, Claude Code, and Gemini Advanced.
